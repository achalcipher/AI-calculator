# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target Audience
Our primary target audience is operations managers, fractional CFOs, and engineering leaders at post-Series A startups (50-200 employees). These companies have usually armed their engineering and growth teams with AI tools (Cursor, ChatGPT, Claude) but have entirely lost track of SaaS sprawl. They are large enough to have significant wastage but small enough that an Operations or Finance lead can unilaterally make a purchasing decision to consolidate or downgrade licenses.

## Getting the First 100 Users for $0

### 1. The "Open Source" Audit Drop
We will leverage high-intent engineering and finance communities (Hacker News, Indie Hackers, r/SaaS, and specific Slack groups like "CFO Connect"). Rather than pitching a service, we will launch Credex as a free "AI Spend Audit Calculator." 

The pitch: *"Are your devs expensing both Cursor Enterprise and ChatGPT Plus? We built a free calculator that tells you exactly how much cash you are burning on redundant AI seats."*

Because the tool does not require an email to see the initial results, it dramatically lowers the barrier to entry and encourages viral sharing. 

### 2. Viral Loop: The Shareable Anonymous URL
The most critical part of our $0 acquisition strategy is the Shareable Anonymous URL feature. When an engineering manager uses the tool and sees they are wasting $12,000 a year, they click "Share Anonymous Audit." This generates an Open Graph-rich link (e.g., "See how this team saved $12,000 on AI"). 

They drop this link into their company Slack or send it to their CFO. The CFO clicks the link, sees the "Mint for AI" dashboard, and is immediately presented with the "Credex Consultation" CTA. This creates a built-in viral loop where one champion inside a company recruits the actual decision-maker organically.

### 3. Cold Outreach via LinkedIn (Highly Targeted)
I will run a highly manual, hyper-targeted cold outreach campaign on LinkedIn. I will search for job titles like "Head of Operations," "Chief Financial Officer," and "VP of Engineering" at companies that recently announced funding. 

The message will be short and value-driven:
*"Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] is scaling fast. Most teams at your stage are overpaying by 30% for redundant AI tools (Cursor, Claude, OpenAI). I built a free calculator that identifies exactly where you can downgrade. No email required to use it: [Link]."*

### 4. Product Hunt Launch
Once the core calculator is polished and we have a few testimonials from our cold outreach, we will launch on Product Hunt. The key to a successful PH launch is the aesthetic—which is why the "Mint for AI" dark mode UI is so critical. We are pitching this as a "beautiful, zero-friction financial tool for the AI era." A top 5 finish on Product Hunt will guarantee a spike of 500-1000 visitors, easily netting us the remainder of our first 100 active leads.
