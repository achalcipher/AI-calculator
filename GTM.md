# 🚀 Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Who are we selling to?
We're going after operations managers, fractional CFOs, and engineering leaders at post-Series A startups (50-200 employees). These companies handed out Cursor and ChatGPT licenses like candy during their growth phase, and now their SaaS sprawl is completely out of control. They are big enough to bleed cash, but small enough that an Ops lead can unilaterally decide to cut the cord.

## How to get the first 100 users for $0

### 1. The "Open Source" Audit Drop
We're not going to pitch a service. We're going to drop a free tool into high-intent communities (Hacker News, Indie Hackers, r/SaaS, CFO Slack groups).
**The hook:** *"Are your devs expensing Cursor Enterprise AND ChatGPT Plus? We built a free calculator to show you exactly how much cash you're burning on redundant AI seats."*
Because we don't gate the results behind an email wall, the friction is zero.

### 2. The Viral Loop (The Secret Weapon)
The most critical feature is the "Share Anonymous Audit" button. 
An engineering manager sees they are wasting $12,000 a year. They click Share. It generates a slick URL with a nice Open Graph preview ("See how this team saved $12k on AI").
They drop that link into their company Slack. The CFO clicks it, sees our gorgeous dashboard, and *bam*—they get hit with the "Book a Consultation" CTA. We use internal champions to recruit the actual decision-makers for us.

### 3. Hyper-Targeted LinkedIn Cold Outreach
I'm going to run a highly manual outreach campaign. Targeting: "Head of Operations," "CFO," and "VP of Engineering" at recently funded companies.
**The message:** *"Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] is scaling fast. Most teams at your stage overpay by 30% for redundant AI tools. I built a free calculator that spots the waste instantly. No email required to use it: [Link]."*

### 4. Product Hunt Launch
Once the core calculator is bulletproof, we launch on Product Hunt. The "Mint for AI" aesthetic is going to carry us here. A top 5 finish on Product Hunt guarantees a massive spike in traffic, easily netting us our first 100 active leads.
